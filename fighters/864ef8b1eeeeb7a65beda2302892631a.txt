						</div><p><div id="attachment_313" class="wp-caption alignright" style="width: 210px"><a href="http://www.bjjheroes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/Alexandre-pimentel.jpg"><img src="http://www.bjjheroes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/Alexandre-pimentel.jpg" alt="Alexandre Pimentel Pulga" title="Alexandre-pimentel" width="200" height="240" class="size-full wp-image-313" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Alexandre Pimentel Pulga</p></div>Alexandre &#8220;Pulga&#8221; Pimentel is an Excellent Jiu Jitsu and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) Fighter out of the Nova Geracao camp in Brazil.<br />
.<br />
<strong>Full Name:</strong> Alexandre Concieiro de Albuquerque Maranhão Pimentel.<br />
.<br />
<strong>Alexandre Pimentel’s Lineage:</strong> Mitsuyo Maeda > <a href="http://www.bjjheroes.com/bjj-fighters/carlos-gracie-sr-profile"> Carlos Gracie</a> > <a href="http://www.bjjheroes.com/bjj-fighters/carlson-gracie-profile">Carlson Gracie</a> > <a href="http://www.bjjheroes.com/bjj-fighters/francisco-toco-albuquerque">Francisco “Toco” Albuquerque</a> > Alexandre Pimentel.<br />
.<br />
<strong>Meaning of Alexandre Pimentel’s Nickname:</strong> “Pulga” means “Flea” in Portuguese, the nickname was given because Alexandre was very small when he was a child, and his friends started calling him “Pulga”.<br />
.<br />
<strong>Alexandre Pimentel’s Main Achievements (BJJ/Grappling/MMA): </strong>World Champion by CBJJE (2009 Lightweight Black Belt Division); South American Champion CBJJE (2006); Brazilian Champion (2006, Lightweight Black Belt Division); Brazilian National Team Champion (2004/2005), Alexandre has also picked up his MMA career starting off with a perfect 9-0.<br />
.<br />
<strong>Alexandre “Pulga” Pimentel’s Weight Category in Jiu Jitsu:</strong> Lightweight.<br />
.<br />
<strong>Alexandre “Pulga”’s Favourite Technique:</strong> Omoplata.<br />
.<br />
<strong>Alexandre Pimentel’s Team/Association:</strong> Nova Geração.</p>
<h2>Alexandre Pulga Pimentel Biography</h2>
<p>Alexandre Pimentel was born on the 2nd of Ocbober 1978 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. His first contact with Jiu Jitsu was after watching the famous Jiu Jitsu vs Luta Livre event, 1991. </p>
<p>A year later as he hung out with a friend who trained BJJ, Alex found that a gym had just opened on his street, he went for a trial class which was his first of many classes with Mestre <a href="http://www.bjjheroes.com/bjj-fighters/francisco-toco-albuquerque">Francisco Albuquerque</a> “Toco”, the man who would later (in 2000) hand Alexandre his Black Belt.</p>
<p>In 2008 came the opportunity to fight MMA, Alexandre had already dwelled with the idea of trying MMA as he was a sparring partner for a few MMA fighters at Nova Geracao, although he never thought about being a professional of the sport. The first fight came and he enjoyed it so much that he went for it again. The contacts to continue fighting kept coming through and Alexandre always took them, before he knew it he was a successful professional MMA fighter.</p>
